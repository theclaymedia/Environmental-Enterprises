Who We Are

One of the Gulf South's largest aquatic toxicity labs

Founded in April 1987 by Harold A. Loyacano, Jr., Ph.D. to perform acute toxicity testing of drilling fluids for the offshore oil and gas industry, EE USA has grown to cover marine and freshwater toxicity, sediment toxicity, biodegradation, oil & grease, GC/MS analysis, data management, and in-house organism culturing.

Our facility — purpose-built in 1995 as an aquatic toxicity testing laboratory — features a private deep-water well, four independent environmental chambers, a fireproof file room, and a backup generator for the entire building. In 2013, founder Harold Loyacano sold the company to David Daniel, a 30-year company veteran and former General Manager.
